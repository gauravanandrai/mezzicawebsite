Our Story

Born in Sicily, Crafted in Toronto

Denise hails from Sicily, Paolo from Abruzzo. We moved to Toronto with one dream: to bring the authentic taste of Italian gelato to Canada.

We traveled back to Italy to source traditional recipes and the finest ingredients — from handpicked pistachios from Bronte to fresh lemons from the Amalfi Coast. Every ingredient tells a story of heritage and authenticity.

Chef Paolo spent years perfecting each recipe — learning from masters in Sicily before bringing that knowledge to Toronto. The result is gelato that tastes exactly like stepping into a gelateria on a sun-soaked Sicilian afternoon.

It's not ice cream. Gelato is churned slower with less air, made with more milk than cream, and more intensely flavored. ;)
